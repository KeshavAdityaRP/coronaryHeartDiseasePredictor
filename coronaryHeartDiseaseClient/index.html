Some Information Regarding the Dataset !

The dataset hold patient's information. It includes roughly around 4,000 Records and 15 Attributes. 14 Attributes are potential risk factors and can be used to predict whether the patient has 10-year risk of future Coronary Heart Disease (CHD)
